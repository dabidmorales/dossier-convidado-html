Synopsis

In a world stripped of color, one artisan fights to remember the shade of gold. The Velvet Curtain follows the fragmented memories of Elias, a costume designer trapped in a looping rehearsal of his masterpiece that never premieres.

As the lines between the script and reality blur, the audience is invited to question: Is the play a tragedy of forgetting, or a triumph of imagination?

Artistic Intent

The Atmosphere

To create a claustrophobic yet infinite space where lighting defines the boundaries of reality.

The Silence

Exploring the weight of unsaid words. The soundscape is minimal, punctuated by raw, diegetic sounds.

The Audience

Breaking the fourth wall not by address, but by shared vulnerability. We are all waiting in the dark.

Staging & Visuals
